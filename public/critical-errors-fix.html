  • Alpine Expression Error: stats is not defined
  • ReferenceError: stats is not defined


Fichier concerné : resources/views/super-admin/dashboard.blade.php (ligne 6)
✅ SOLUTION
Corriger la syntaxe x-data :

<div class="space-y-8" x-data="{ stats: {!! json_encode($stats ?? []) !!} }">
